How to Apply for Scholarship

  1. Scholarship Eligibility Criteria are mentioned in the relevant Course Page on our website..
  2. Prescribed Scholarship Application Form can be obtained from our Office.
  3. Scholarship request made verbally or through phone/email/fax shall not be entertained in any case.
  4. To avail the scholarship, student has to produce required Documents in Original for Verification & submit Photocopy (as mentioned below) along with the Scholarship Application form at the earliest at our Corporate Office (KOTA) / Classroom Center Office.
  5. Last date to submit Scholarship Application Form is 30/09/2019. No application for scholarship thereafter shall be accepted in any case.

Important Information :

  1. Students of whom Result of X Board / XII Board / NEET 2019 / JEE Main 2019 / Jee Advanced 2019 is awaited at the time of Admission can also Apply for Scholarship at later stage (before last date) with the below mentioned documents, if eligible.
  2. In case of Applying for Scholarship after taking Admission you can avail scholarship either:
    a. At the time of paying Second Installment, or
    b. By applying for Scholarship Disbursement.
  3. Disbursement of Scholarship will be credited in Father / Mother / Student Bank Account by September 2019 or 1 month from the date of Application (whichever is later) through NEFT / RTGS only. For this you have to submit cancelled cheque or bank passbook first page copy along with scholarship form.
  4. Scholarship provided on the basis of Class X / Class XII (result declared in 2019) will be provided in Current Academic Session (2019-20) only.
  5. Scholarship provided on the basis of NEET (UG) 2019 / JEE (Main) 2019/ Jee (Advanced) 2019 will be provided only in Current Academic Session (2019-20) only.
  6. Scholarship amount will be calculated on Total Amount.
  7. Scholarship for Sibling (TS24, MS24, ES24, and PS10) will be available only for one candidate (real brother/sister).

A student is eligible for only one scholarship at a time, No two criteria can be clubbed to avail deduction in fee at Domain E.
