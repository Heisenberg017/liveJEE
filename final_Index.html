About Conquer JEE 3D

The NCERT Textbooks are the most authentic source for conceptual understanding and focussed coverage of syllabi of Class XI/XII. Though the Textbooks are one of the most prolific works by experts, but in many cases, some students find the book content tough to comprehend. In Conquer Series the endeavour is to restructure the content in such a way that understanding of concepts and memorization, wherever required, becomes easier for the students, without compromising on the authenticity of the Content.

Digital Content

On top of it Conquer Series have added Video Lectures of selected topics, Animations & Digital 3D Models to enhance the learning Experience using a Mobile App. The topics for the same have been carefully selected to overcome the limitation of learning through books, in cases, where the comprehension of complex concepts, visualization of processes & 3D structures are required for a better understanding.

Lessons with Solved Examples

The most comprehensive notes for all the topics. All Proofs and Derivations are given separately. Lots of solved examples.
Problem Sets:

  1. Exercise I - NCERT Exercise
  2. Exercise II - Board/Scholl Exam Oriented
  3. Flashback I - Topic wise segregation of last 10 years CBSE Exam Problems.
