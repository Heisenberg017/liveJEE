Steps To Your Live Classes   
STEP  1
Course Chooser
  • Choose your course from Live Coaching Courses.
  • On ticking a box you get an invoice as a pop-up.
  • You may keep on adding or deleting course.
  • After final selection click 'Pay Now'.
STEP  2
Profile Detail
  • Fill your personal details in the given form.
  • Fields marked with * are compulsory.
  • Then proceed to Transaction page where you may pay through Payment gateway OR through Online banking transaction.
STEP  3
Make Payment
  • Once you fill the form, it will show your details & the selection done by you.
  • Kindly re-check the data.
  • Choose the mode through which you want to pay the fees. If you want to pay through debit or credit card, select 'CC Avenue' option. If you want to pay through NEFT Bank transaction, choose 'Bank details'.
  • Once you choose your mode of payment, it will guide you further.
STEP  4
Final Registration
  • Once you make payment, you will receive a 'Welcome mail' from our end.
  • We will mail you your receipt and enrolment form.
  • Once we receive the filled enrollment form from your side, the registration process will be completed.
  • We will despatch your study material after completing the registration process.
STEP  5
Orientation Class
  • You get an Orientation class for Do's and Don't for the classes.
  • Suggestions for the optimum use of Internet.
  • How to study for the effective output.
